    David A. Parra

Having grown up throughout Latin America, David Parra is the founding partner of Greenway Studio in El Paso, Texas. He earned his Masters of Environmental Planning from Macquarie University in Sydney, Australia, and his Bachelors of Landscape Architecture from Texas Tech University. He is passionate about Landscape Architecture and enjoys global experiences that bring a rich and varied background to his work.

David's collaborative approach and dedication ensures a fully integrated landscape. His work throughout the southwest brings a deep understanding of water conservation practices and sustainable design. From the design process all the way through construction, David's attention to detail, keen aesthetic, and experience in the field allow him to create and deliver innovative landscape design on any scale.

    Luis Fernando Terrazas
  • Landscape Architect, RLA, LI, CNU-A
  • BLA Texas Tech University
  • luis@greenway-studio.com
  • Nada

As principal of Greenway Studio, Luis shares a combined experience of over ten years in the landscape architecture industry. He is a Texas Tech graduate (a Red Raider at heart!) with his Bachelors in Landscape Architecture. Luis influences and inspires others in desert southwest landscape best practices. His attention to detail and intuition play a key role in site-sensitive design, striking a harmonious balance between social and environmental design.

Luis is especially capable of managing projects of any scale and across multiple jurisdictions. He completely transforms landscapes while delivering health, safety, and welfare-conscious design. He promotes sustainable design through the combination of native landscape materials and the introduction of water harvesting techniques. As a Texas Licensed Irrigator, Luis adds a wealth of knowledge and hands-on experience that assures efficiency throughout the process.
