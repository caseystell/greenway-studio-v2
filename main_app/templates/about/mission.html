PEOPLE, MEET NATURE

Work at Greenway Studio begins with a simple idea:
uniting people with nature.

  For us, this isn't only our passion; we see it as our responsibility.

We understand that in modern urban life, spaces for experiencing nature are limited. Not only does Greenway Studio provide opportunities, we make the most of them by creating delightful surprises in interacting with nature.

  We offer customized solutions using a methodical approach.

Greenway Studio realizes that every project is unique, yet we approach each one the same way. Every landscape is evaluated in reference to its surroundings, the needs of the community, individual budget and project constraints, and the preservation of the culture of the desert southwest. While balancing conflicting needs can be a challenge, Greenway Studio finds ways to add value to each project and delivers innovative solutions every time.

  We strive to make a lasting, sustainable impact.

Sustainability is not just a buzzword to us; it's a way of being. We pride ourselves on creating meaningful landscapes that make a positive environmental impact and won't drain our precious regional resources. We are experienced in the use of local remediation landscape, including drought tolerant plants, native grasses, and various hydromulch techniques that contribute to a sustainable American Southwestern landscape.

  We maintain versatility while holding ourselves to the highest standards.

Greenway Studio strives to develop healthy places where people live, work, play, shop, and experience vibrant landscape design. Our designs demonstrate a deep understanding of environmental planning, sustainable development, new urbanism principles, water harvesting techniques, and best practices in the field.
